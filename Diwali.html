What's Deepawali?

It's the best festival!
Deepawali symbolizes the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance. It is also a time to worship deities such as Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth, and Ganesha, the god of wisdom and good fortune.
